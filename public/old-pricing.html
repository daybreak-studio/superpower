  • Book services such as MRIs, DEXA scans, microbiome tests, IV drips, blood tests, & consults
  • Get advice about the best food and products to buy. Send us a photo of a menu or nutrition label
  • Microbiome Ask questions about your biomarkers tested with Superpower
  • Manage logistics around scheduling and product refills
  • Check any front-of-mind health problems you are having
  • Learn about products on our marketplace
Can I get more tests whenever I want?

Yes.

We’re here to support all of your preventative health, performance and longevity needs. A core part of the Superpower Concierge is being able to get you access to all the testing you need, in minutes. That includes custom lab panels, MRIs, DEXA scans, Galleri’s cancer screen, and more.

Do you replace my primary care doctor?

No. Your annual membership to Superpower’s Baseline testing and health concierge is designed to complement your primary care doctor, not replace them.

We do not offer typical sick care, but rather, our longevity physicians are there to answer questions related to prevention, performance, and longevity. We try to step in where the healthcare system is falling short. We provide best-in-class early diagnostics assessments and guidance from our longevity-trained physicians.

Where do I go for my lab visit?

You will be tested at one of over 2,000 locations across the US, within 10 miles of nearly every home in the United States. Our lab partner performs over 50 million tests per year and is trusted by the entire medical community. We also offer at-home testing for an additional fee where a nurse will come to you.

Does Superpower accept health insurance?

Not at this time, but we’re working on it. We already accept HSA and FSA.

We see Superpower like a gym membership for those committed to prevention and performance. Superpower is a bridge between wellness and healthcare. Health insurance traditionally focuses on reactive care whereas, at Superpower, we believe it’s never too early to start looking out for your long-term health.

Which tests are included in my membership?

The Superpower Baseline includes two lab panels.

The first lab panel includes over 60 biomarkers. You can find the full list of biomarkers here.

The second lab panel is a follow-up test with over 35 biomarkers.

Additional tests can be ordered at any time from the Superpower app. We have brought together the best tests into one platform. Many of these are hard to access anywhere else.

Our price guarantee means that for all items in our curated marketplace, we will price match.

We take data privacy and information security extremely seriously. Superpower is designed to be your lifelong health partner and give you full control and ownership of your health information.
